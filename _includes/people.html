Gregor McPherson

He specialises in:

  • designing business solutions to improve organisational capability
  • designing new products/services
  • solving business problems

Gregor draws on 15 years of experience in senior roles across industries and disciplines — from UK central government departments to an international shipping port, from telecoms to automotive, legal services to professional membership bodies, and many more in between.

He brings a unique blend of commercial acumen, psychology, and technical expertise across numerous specialisations, and experience in delivering business results through challenging periods of growth, redevelopment, and change. He has worked in commercial, operational, project, and programme delivery capacities across the private and public sectors.

Career highlights include:
  • Programme Director responsible for Supplier Management, Contract Management, Programme and Project Management, PMO and IT infrastructure within the Civil Service Learning service
  • Solution design, definition, and implementation of a leadership programme for the franchise network of 116 centres for Audi UK
  • Recruitment and Training (technical and soft skills) for the Brand Experience team of Audi City. All work carried out under full secrecy, delivered successfully, on time, within budget
  • Culture change and business transformation programme design and implementation across the 3,500 strong operations function at Port of Felixstowe
  • Programme management, solution design, implementation, and operational delivery of training services for the 2011 Census
